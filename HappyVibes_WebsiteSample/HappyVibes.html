About Us

We are a Canadian not-for-profit organization with the goal to connect young people with mental health resources. Our unapologetic bold, fun and vibrant approach comes from our deep belief that there is hope in the dark places. We want to empower those struggling with mental health issues with knowledge, connect them with mentors and coaches, and help them rediscover their “Happy Vibes.”
